The person behind the app

Dave
Creator · Match Tracker Pro

I grew up in Houston and picked up tennis in my early teens, then life happened. After retiring from most other recreational sports and settling down in the Atlanta suburbs, I spotted my neighborhood team playing one day and remembered how much fun the game was, so I jumped back in.

A couple years later I was playing in multiple leagues at once and my scores were scattered across different websites, apps, and my own memory. I wanted to know how I was actually performing in a given season, with specific partners, in pressure situations, etc. but every tool I found was built for high-level players or coaches, not for someone like me.

So I built Match Tracker Pro. Not as a product at first, just something I wanted for myself. A clean way for recreational players to finally see what their numbers mean to their game. If you play in local leagues and have ever wanted to know your actual stats without a spreadsheet degree, this was made for you.
